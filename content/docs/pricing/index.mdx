How much does WiseChat cost? WiseChat offer many pricing plan but you can start using it for free. You can easily build a usable chatbot for free but you'll have to upgrade to a higher plan if you want customizations.

Here's our four pricing plans:

Free Plan: Includes 1 chatbot, 1 crawler, 3 files, 500 messages, and support for $0 per month.

Hobby Plan: Includes 3 chatbot, 3 crawler, 9 files, unlimited messages, customization, and support for $3 per month.

Basic Plan: Includes 9 chatbots , 9 crawlers, 27 files, unlimited messages, customization, and premium support for $9 per month.

Pro Plan: Includes 27 chatbots, 27 crawlers, 81 files, unlimited messages, customization, and premium support for $27 per month.

With the customization option you can remove the branding "Powered by WiseChat", change the widget color, and change the chat bubbles color.
